# Account Deletion Policy & Instructions - Murranno Music

This document outlines the procedures for requesting the deletion of your Murranno Music account and associated data, as required by the Google Play Store and global data protection regulations.

## 1. How to Request Account Deletion

### Option A: In-App (Fastest)
If you have access to your account, you can initiate deletion directly:
1. Log into the Murranno Music app or website.
2. Navigate to **Settings**.
3. Scroll down to the **Danger Zone** section.
4. Click **Delete Account**.
5. Confirm your decision. Your account will be queued for immediate deletion.

### Option B: Web Request (No Login Required)
If you cannot access your account, you can submit a request via our support team:
*   **Support Email:** support@murrannomusic.co
*   **Request Format:** Please send the email from the address associated with your account with the subject line "Account Deletion Request - [Your Name]".

---

## 2. Data Deletion Scope
When an account deletion request is processed, Murranno Music performs the following actions:

*   **Personal Identity:** Your name, email, phone number, and profile image are permanently removed from our active databases.
*   **Content & Music:** All audio files, album artwork, and song metadata uploaded to Murranno Music are deleted from our storage servers.
*   **Distribution:** We will issue "Takedown" requests to all Digital Service Providers (DSPs) such as Spotify, Apple Music, and Tidal to remove your content from their platforms.
*   **Financial Records:** Transaction history and earnings data are archived for a period of 7 years to comply with Nigerian tax and anti-money laundering (AML) laws. After this period, the data is permanently destroyed.

---

## 3. Timeline for Deletion
*   **In-App Requests:** Account access is disabled immediately. Data deletion from our primary servers typically completes within 24-48 hours.
*   **DSP Takedowns:** While we send takedown notices immediately, streaming platforms may take between 2 to 4 weeks to fully remove content from their public libraries.
*   **Backup Retention:** Residual data may remain in our encrypted backups for up to 30 days before being completely overwritten.
